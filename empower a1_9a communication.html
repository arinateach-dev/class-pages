✏️ Writing task

✍️
Write about a real or imaginary holiday (8–10 sentences)
Where did you go? How did you travel? Where did you stay? What did you do? What did you eat?

Use past simple. Try to include: went, flew / took a train, stayed, ate, took photos, bought, liked.

📖 Vocabulary task

📓
Learn 10 irregular verbs
Write the infinitive → past simple → translation in your vocabulary notebook.
Start with: go/went, fly/flew, eat/ate, buy/bought, take/took, see/saw, have/had, come/came, give/gave, get/got.
